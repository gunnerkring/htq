# Installing Hours to Qualify (Unsigned Build)

These builds are free to distribute, but they are not code signed yet. Your computer may warn you the first time you open them.

## macOS

1. Download the macOS `.zip` or `.dmg`.
2. Move `Hours to Qualify.app` into `Applications`.
3. Try to open the app once.
4. If macOS blocks it, either:
   - right-click the app and choose `Open`, then confirm, or
   - go to `System Settings > Privacy & Security` and allow the app there.

After the first approval, the app should open normally.

## Windows

1. Download the Windows installer `.exe` or portable `.exe`.
2. Open the file.
3. If Microsoft Defender SmartScreen warns you, click `More info`.
4. Click `Run anyway`.

After that, the app should install or launch normally.

## Why this warning appears

The app is not yet signed with Apple Developer ID or Windows code-signing certificates. The warning is expected for free unsigned builds.
